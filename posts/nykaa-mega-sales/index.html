Nykaa Mega Sales Conversion Optimization

May 24, 2025

Company Background

Nykaa is a leading beauty and wellness e-commerce platform in India, offering a wide range of products from top brands. With a valuation of $6 billion and a successful IPO on the Bombay Stock Exchange, Nykaa has become a household name in the Indian market.

Project Context

In 2016, as the Head of Product and Analytics at Nykaa, I was tasked with optimizing the user journey during high-traffic Mega Sales events. During these sales, traffic typically spiked 4-5x, but we noticed a 1% drop in conversion rates.

Key insights:

  • Only 30% of visitors landed on the designated Sales Page, which had 3x higher conversion rates.
  • A significant portion of users were spending 5+ minutes browsing without adding items to their carts.

The Challenge

How can we guide these browsing users to the high-converting Sales Page during a Mega Sale, without disrupting their experience?

The Solution

We implemented a subtle bottom slider targeting users who had:

  • Spent over 5 minutes browsing.
  • Viewed multiple product pages but hadn‚Äôt added items to their cart.

The slider message:

‚ÄúYou are missing out on buying your favorite product at 50%! Click here to know how.‚Äù

The slider linked directly to the Sales Page, leveraging the urgency of the sale and the user’s browsing behavior.

The Results

  • 65% Click-Through Rate (CTR) on the slider, compared to a standard 10% CTR for similar messages.
  • Traffic to the Sales Page increased from 30% to 50% of total visitors.
  • Overall sales revenue increased by 10% during the Mega Sale.

Long-term Impact

The success of this simple nudge led to its adoption as a standard practice for all major sales campaigns. Eventually, Nykaa even made the Sales Page the default homepage during Mega Sales events.

Key Learnings

  1. User behavior during sales differs drastically from regular browsing patterns.
  2. Timely, targeted UI nudges can significantly lift conversions.
  3. Continuous testing and iteration are critical for optimizing e-commerce sales funnels.
